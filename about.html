About BH Web Solutions

Beautiful, affordable websites for businesses of all sizes and for private people — and beyond.

A Brooklyn-based web design studio building fast, modern websites and online stores for kosher businesses, frum entrepreneurs, mosdos and local service companies — with a real person behind every build.

01 — Who we are

Not an agency.Not a template shop.

A hands-on web developer who treats your website like it's our own — not a faceless agency, not an overseas template shop.

  • Based in Brooklyn, NY

    Serving clients everywhere — distance is never a problem.

  • Stores, WordPress & custom platforms

    From clean landing pages to full e-commerce and portals.

  • English & Yiddish, done right

    Fully bilingual builds where the copy actually makes sense.

BH Web Solutions started with a simple idea: that a frum business owner shouldn't have to choose between a website that looks cheap and one that costs a fortune. Too many heimishe businesses are stuck with slow, dated sites — or pay big-agency prices for work they could get done better, faster, and friendlier.

So we build the in-between that should have existed all along: genuinely premium websites at a price that makes sense for a growing business. Every project is handled personally — you talk directly to the person designing and coding your site, not a sales rep or a support queue.

We've built large kosher online stores, custom ordering platforms with wallets and member accounts, elegant catering and simcha sites, nonprofit and mosad websites, and clean local-business sites that turn visitors into phone calls. From a simple landing page to a full e-commerce build, we make it look great, load fast, and work flawlessly on every phone.

And because we build many sites as fast static pages with free hosting, a lot of our clients pay nothing in monthly hosting bills — a real, lasting saving most web designers never offer.

05 — FAQ

Frequently askedquestions.

A few of the things people ask before starting a project with us.

Do you build websites for kosher and Jewish businesses?

Yes — it's our specialty. We build websites and online stores for kosher stores, caterers, bakeries, sefarim and Judaica shops, mosdos and frum-owned businesses, and we understand the community you're serving.

How much does a website cost?

Less than you'd expect for this quality. Pricing depends on the size of the project — a simple landing page costs far less than a full online store. We give honest, fair quotes up front with no surprises. Order now and we'll get back to you quickly.

How long does it take to build a site?

Most sites go live in days, not months. Larger online stores and custom platforms take a bit longer, and we'll always tell you the timeline up front.

Can you build an online store with checkout?

Absolutely. We've built online stores with large catalogs, wholesale ordering portals, PayPal and card checkout, and custom platforms with prepaid wallets and member accounts. See examples in our work.

Can you move my existing WordPress site so I stop paying for hosting?

Yes. We migrate WordPress sites to fast static hosting that's faster, more secure, and often free to host — so you keep your site and lose the monthly bills.

Where are you located, and do you work remotely?

We're based in Brooklyn, NY and serve businesses across Williamsburg, Boro Park, Monsey, Lakewood and beyond. Since everything is done online, we happily work with clients anywhere in the country.
