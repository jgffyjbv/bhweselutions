BH Web Solutions started with a simple idea: that a frum business owner shouldn't have to choose between a website that looks cheap and one that costs a fortune. Too many heimishe businesses are stuck with slow, dated sites — or pay big-agency prices for work they could get done better, faster, and friendlier.

So we build the in-between that should have existed all along: genuinely premium websites at a price that makes sense for a growing business. Every project is handled personally — you talk directly to the person designing and coding your site, not a sales rep or a support queue.

We've built large kosher online stores with hundreds of products, custom ordering platforms with wallets and member accounts, elegant catering and simcha sites, nonprofit and mosad websites, and clean local-business sites that turn visitors into phone calls. From a one-page landing site to a full e-commerce build, we make it look great, load fast, and work flawlessly on every phone.

And because we build many sites as fast static pages with free hosting, a lot of our clients pay nothing in monthly hosting bills — a real, lasting saving most web designers never offer.

Frequently asked questions

A few of the things people ask before starting a project with us.

Q.Do you build websites for kosher and Jewish businesses?

Yes — it's our specialty. We build websites and online stores for kosher stores, caterers, bakeries, sefarim and Judaica shops, mosdos and frum-owned businesses, and we understand the community you're serving.

Q.How much does a website cost?

Less than you'd expect for this quality. Pricing depends on the size of the project — a clean one-page site costs far less than a full online store. We give honest, fair quotes up front with no surprises. Request a quote and we'll get back to you within hours.

Q.How long does it take to build a site?

Most sites go live within days — our typical turnaround is around 48 hours from start to launch for a standard build. Larger online stores and custom platforms take a bit longer, and we'll always tell you the timeline up front.

Q.Can you build an online store with checkout?

Absolutely. We've built online stores with hundreds of products, wholesale ordering portals, PayPal and card checkout, and custom platforms with prepaid wallets and member accounts. See examples in our work.

Q.Can you move my existing WordPress site so I stop paying for hosting?

Yes. We migrate WordPress sites to fast static hosting that's faster, more secure, and often free to host — so you keep your site and lose the monthly bills.

Q.Where are you located, and do you work remotely?

We're based in Brooklyn, NY and serve businesses across Williamsburg, Boro Park, Monsey, Lakewood and beyond. Since everything is done online, we happily work with clients anywhere in the country.
